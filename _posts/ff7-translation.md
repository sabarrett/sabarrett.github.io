---
title: Final Fantasy 7's Translation Is Good Enough
layout: post
---

FF7's translation feels a little clunky. While it actually does communicate the
points of the plot well, the occasional typo or oddly phrased line of dialogue
still manages to affect the game in a significant way. It undermines the
confidence of the player that they are being given true information. When
something confusing happens, instead of wondering "Wow, I wonder where the
writer is going with this!", the first thought it "Is that what that text box
was supposed to say?"
